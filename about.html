About Glorious Vision University

Empowering minds, shaping futures, and driving innovation in technology education.

Our Story

Glorious Vision University, a tertiary institution of The Apostolic Church Nigeria, LAWNA having Edo and Lagos Campuses in operation. Glorious Vision University is an Institution established to raise knowledge driven and godly generation that will redefine the future of the world. Glorious Vision University came into existence through prophetic ministration on January 1, 2005. Subsequently, the University Core Committee (UCC) was inaugurated by the Proprietor of the University, The Apostolic Church, LAWNA Territory in September, 2005 and charged with the responsibility of setting up a university for the Church.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To be a world class university, excellent in academic traditions and exceptional in providing university education which empowers the graduates to act with professional competence and ethical steadfastness in fulfilling their obligations to the society.

Vision

To provide wholesome and comprehensive education for intellectual and spiritual development of her students in preparation for leadership roles and service to the society.
